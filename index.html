About Me

Hi, I'm Raphael Lamboloto, the CEO of IBL Productions, a Photography and Filmmaking company dedicated to capturing moments that show raw emotion, and treasured memories you cannot undo. At a young age, I have been passionate about turning my ideas into a reality. Whether that may be a music idea turned into a song, or a beautiful landscape captured through the lens of a camera.

The goal of this company is to create visual content that not only looks stunning but also tells a story and evokes emotion.

Frequently Asked Questions

What photography styles do you offer?

In Between Lines Productions offers portrait, sports, landscape, and wedding photography services.

How do I book a shoot?

Shoot out a DM on my instagram to get in touch and book a session.

What is your equipment/What Camera do you use

I use a Sony A6400, which is a great entry-level mirrorless camera for both photography and videography. Along with a Insta360 X3 for 360-degree video, and a Go Pro Hero 13 for action shots. Microphone-wise, I use the Rode VideoMic Pro, and the DJI Mic 2. And lastly, I use a DJI RS3 Gimbal for stabilizing the camera during filming.

What is your typical turnaround time?

Most projects are delivered within a week after the shoot, but if you pay 300$ more, I will make it same day, depending on editing requirements.
